Horror Shop Games, helmed by jatzy, is an independent game studio which features cute girls and their horrifying experiences, with exceptional soundtrack added to the mix. this is where the cuteness and care becomes contagious-- and scary.

Mother (not a hit indie game of the same name), released in 2019, focuses on a mother named Agatha taking care of her sons during the course of winter. as hunger is foreseen, she is forced to decide the fate of such... until one of the brothers know the secrets and tries to stop her with the best he could.
