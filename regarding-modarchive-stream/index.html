Regarding on streaming Mod Archive music...

Updated 7 Gen 2021

Katherine's Test Streams are one way of listening and replaying all of the music posted in The Mod Archive, Since 3 December 2020, we have been listening to hours of songs posted on the archive, with more every day. And since 6 January 2021, our listening never stops with Kohina.

Unfortunately, during our streams, there are some modules from TMA that 'slipped through the cracks', leading to some of our streams being claimed by copyright, while others being blocked in other countries.

Due to this, we will be taking all measures needed to prevent this from happening. Here are our measures taken effect starting 7 January 2021:


I--thebelovedmoon--will find those modules in an explicit manual manner. And if that module matches the criteria, I will try to contact the TMA Staff (most notably Saga Musix) to report the modules against copyright infringement (as per TMA's Upload Terms, Conditions and License Agreement).

The criteria will be as follows: Exact sample data from the original variant, samples which are derived from the original but with modifications (unless considered), and modules made entirely of full-length sample data.

However, there are things that may be considered while reviewing the affected modules: If the note data is made entirely from scratch, or is a remix of the original variant.

If the modules reported are deleted, we (Katherine and I) will begin cutting the affected segment from the stream without further notice.


Additional disclosure:

As per Section 107 of the Copyright Act of 1976, allowance is made for "fair use" for purposes such as criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, education and research.

Fair use is a use permitted by copyright statute that might otherwise be infringing. Non-profit, educational or personal use tips the balance in favor of fair use.

No copyright infringement intended, unless otherwise noted with prior notice.


Thank you for sticking around to her Test Streams, and we hope to make more soon.
