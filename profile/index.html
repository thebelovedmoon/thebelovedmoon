Joshua started his life on the internet since 2014 with a simple passion of interacting to verious amounts of people of age and race. Although his hiatus in 2016 was apparent with his responsibilities in life, it wasn't until 2017 when his interest in gaming came in his universe. Since then, he became active, without knowing his legacy of being a roleplayer (as most people call it) came to light. He has managed multiple accounts and has gained traction on his participating communities, most notably the Dark Parables and DDLC (Doki Doki Literature Club).

He noted that his legacy of socialization is already his habit, not until some hurdles came in his way. A prime example is his involvement in a series of commotions with a group of people that he wanted to socialize at first, but the sheer amount of extreme pressure has left him a fatal blow on his internet life; Giugno 2019 is when his entire desire of socialization went into the edge, and he became silent, just trying his best to get back on track.

He moved out of the Philippines on Agosto 2019 in order to start over, together with a bunch of girls whom their promise is to take care of whom they destined to be with. They founded The Moon 🌙 which in turn translated to Italian months later: La Luna 🌙, in which they considered him as The Beloved 🌙. Aside from the incorporation, their daily task is to get connected while taking precautionary measures to ensure that his mental conditions won't deteriorate over time. Up to this day, La Luna 🌙 is situated on the Matese Massif at 81010 Italia.

He decided to start back from scratch, and on Novembre 2020, he re-incepted as projectElyssa (a reference to projectLibitina), which turned out to be his canvas for incorporating a lot of communities under his belt. He currently juggles between DDLC and DOA (Dead or Alive) Community, with his greatest contribution on the other being #MamaNyoSquad.

He enjoys a bit of gaming with DOAXVV being on his top priority, and his interactions made it even look like he has to reveal his big return. (It was this revamped website that announces his return to socialization era.)

He is also experimenting with various aspects involving music and video, with the latest being his Synthwave scene. He is the head producer for La Luna Music and LunaSynth.
