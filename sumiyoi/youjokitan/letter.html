Updated 18 July 2022

If you are reading this, the game has officially shut down. To reiterate:

- 25 May 2022 marks the start of the 「End of Service」 event where paid items cannot be purchased. Moreover, Paid Sakura Stones cannot be used.
- 30 June 2022 is where Fusion Studio pulls the plug on the game, rendering it officially offline.
- From 30 June to 29 September 2022, they are starting to issue refunds on Paid Sakura Stones. Note that this might apply to some clients.

For more info on these notices, head over to their website (in Japanese).
